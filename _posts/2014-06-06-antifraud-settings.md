Just adjusted settings for anti-fraud fights:

* increased min. spend is 15 USD (for all bookings for everyone atm)
* decreased max. number of friend-redeem vouchers from 40 to 10 which will trigger "Possible fraud" => cancelled booking

These are quick fixes (thanks to Abhi & Gin for putting lots of thoughts into it), more improvements after release.
